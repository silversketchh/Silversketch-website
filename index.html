WHO I AM

I am Silver Sketch — a self-taught artist from Tamil Nadu, India. No classes. No formal training. Just a pencil, paper, and years of obsession with detail.

I draw what I love — supercars, superbikes, machines, and powerful subjects. Every artwork is done entirely by hand, from reference to finished piece.

Currently studying Civil Engineering at VIT Vellore while building this art journey one drawing at a time.
